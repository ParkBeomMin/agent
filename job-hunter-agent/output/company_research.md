## Company Overview

Krew is a San Francisco-based FinTech startup focused on transforming credit-servicing through AI-powered credit-servicing agents. The company builds omnichannel AI agents that assist over 100,000 consumers in managing their financial wellness by addressing outstanding consumer delinquencies. Krew has a strong backing from notable investors such as Long Journey Ventures and several high-profile angel investors including Ryan Hoover (Founder of Product Hunt) and Charlie Songhurst (Board Member at Meta). The team is composed of experienced engineers and researchers from diverse prestigious backgrounds including robotics, finance, academia, and international organizations.

The company is relatively small with around 11-50 employees, indicating a startup environment with a close-knit, talent-dense team. Krew emphasizes rapid innovation and collaboration across functional teams.

**Mission:**  
Krew’s mission is to resolve more than $1 trillion in outstanding consumer delinquencies worldwide through empathetic, human-centric AI-operated financial wellness solutions. The company aims to empower credit-servicing teams with the most advanced AI technology to enhance consumer pathways to financial health.
